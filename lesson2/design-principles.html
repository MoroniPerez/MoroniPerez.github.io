Design Principles

Repetition

One of the most relevant websites for language learning Duolingo uses a very marked and repetitive design pattern which gives it a very nice and entertaining appearance.

Proximity

Proximity is well used at Amazon to group products with their respective description and prizes. You can understand easy the different products and all their info and that is for well use of proximity.

White Space

The internet's most famous search engine, Google applies the principles of white space keeping the main page clean enhancing a text box to start a search. sometimes simple looks better.
